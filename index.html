About BCBS Values Platform

The BCBS Values Platform leverages cutting-edge artificial intelligence and machine learning technology to provide accurate property valuations for Benton County, Washington.

Our platform combines multiple valuation models, including regression analysis, hedonic pricing, comparable sales, and ML ensemble methods to deliver valuations with high confidence scores.

With a focus on Benton County real estate, we provide specific insights for neighborhoods including West Richland, Kennewick, Pasco, Finley, and Richland.
